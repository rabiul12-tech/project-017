Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Gaggle.fun

What do I need to play?

To play games on Gaggle.fun, you'll need a stable internet connection, a device (computer, tablet, or smartphone), and a web browser. Some games may require our desktop app for the best experience. For voice/video chat, a microphone (and optionally a webcam) is recommended.

Can I play on my phone or tablet?

Yes! Most of our games are responsive and work on mobile devices. We have dedicated apps for iOS and Android for an optimized mobile experience. Some complex escape rooms might be better enjoyed on a computer with a larger screen.

Do all my friends need to buy a game key to play?

It depends on the game. For free games, no purchase is needed. For premium escape rooms and party games, typically only the host needs to purchase a game key, which allows them to invite friends to play together. Some premium games may require each player to have their own key.

What is online escape rooms?

Our online escape rooms are virtual adventures where you and your friends work together to solve puzzles, find clues, and complete challenges to "escape" within a time limit. They feature immersive storylines, interactive elements, and teamwork challenges - all from the comfort of your home.

How do voice/video rooms work?

Our voice/video chat rooms are integrated directly into the gaming experience. When you create or join a game, you'll automatically be connected to the voice chat. You can enable video if you prefer face-to-face interaction. Our technology ensures clear communication with minimal lag, and you can adjust settings or mute yourself at any time.

What is Gaggle.fun?

Who are we?

Gaggle.fun is created by a team of game enthusiasts and technology experts who believe in the power of play to bring people together. Based in San Francisco, our diverse team includes game designers, software engineers, and community managers.

Why did we create Gaggle.fun?

We started Gaggle.fun to solve a simple problem: it's hard to have fun with friends when you're apart. We wanted to create a platform where distance doesn't matter - where you can laugh, play, and make memories together no matter where you are in the world.

What makes us special?

Unlike other gaming platforms, we focus specifically on social experiences. Our games are designed from the ground up to encourage interaction, teamwork, and laughter. We combine high-quality game design with seamless voice/video integration for a truly social experience.
